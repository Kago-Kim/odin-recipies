Simple Egg Salad


Description

This egg salad with ranch dressing is simple but still delicious! Great for leftover Easter eggs.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • Â¼ cup chopped cucumber
  • 3 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix together eggs, cucumber, ranch dressing, mustard, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
